Call for Workshops and Tutorials

CGO 2017 will host co-located workshops and tutorials on Saturday and Sunday before the main conference. This is your event's chance to take advantage of the interdisciplinary audience of CGO, HPCA, and PPoPP. Please submit a proposal!

How to Submit a Proposal

A workshop or tutorial proposal should contain:

  • The title of the workshop or tutorial.
  • The organizers (and their affiliations).
  • A short description of the topic.
  • Full day or half day?
  • Expected attendance. (Please include attendance from any previous iterations.)
  • Any special logistical requirements.

Please email your proposal as a PDF or plain text to the workshop and tutorial chair, Adrian Sampson. If you're thinking of proposing something but aren't sure yet, please get in touch with Adrian anyway. The earlier, the better. The deadline for proposals is August 26, 2016.

Timeline

  • The proposal deadline is August 26, 2016.
  • We will send notifications by September 9, 2016.
  • CGO workshops and tutorials will take place on Saturday and Sunday,February 4 and 5, 2017.
